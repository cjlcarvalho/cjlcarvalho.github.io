---
title: "Call for Mentors (and Admins) for Season of KDE 2021"
date: "2020-09-10"
---

As discussed in the Season of KDE/Google Summer of Code BoF that we had yesterday in [Akademy 2020](https://akademy.kde.org/2020/), our plan is to start the next edition of Season of KDE soon. But first we need to start thinking about some project ideas and, most importantly, **we need mentors**! So if you are a KDE contributor and want to participate as a mentor in the next SoK, please join us and fill the ideas page.

Ideas Page: [https://community.kde.org/SoK/Ideas/2021](https://community.kde.org/SoK/Ideas/2021)

Another important detail is that we need some Season of KDE admins to help to organize the program. If you want to contribute with that, please let me know.
