Artificial Intelligence Researcher from Salvador, Bahia, Brazil. Graduated in Systems Analysis and Development at [Federal Institute of Education, Science and Technology of Bahia (IFBA)](). Current Artificial Intelligence Researcher at [Supercomputing Center for Industrial Innovation – SENAI CIMATEC](), where I have been developing R&D projects in the areas of artificial intelligence and high processing computing. Research Member of Computational Modeling and Intelligence Research Group (GPMIC) and [LABRASOFT](). My research is mainly focused on machine learning, computer vision and time-series analysis. Member of [KDE Community](), where I am the maintainer of [marK](), a dataset annotation tool, and have been contributing to Calamares installer framework, kpmcore partitioning library and KDE Partition Manager.
