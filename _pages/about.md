Artificial Intelligence Researcher from Salvador, Bahia, Brazil. Graduated in Systems Analysis and Development at [Federal Institute of Education, Science and Technology of Bahia (IFBA)](https://portal.ifba.edu.br/).

Current Machine Learning Engineer at [Jusbrasil](https://www.jusbrasil.com.br), the largest legal platform in the world, where I have been working mainly with processing massive datasets and integrating artificial intelligence algorithms to analyze textual documents.

Research Member of [LABRASOFT](https://labrasoft.ifba.edu.br/). My research is mainly focused on machine learning, computer vision and time-series analysis.

Member of [KDE Community](https://kde.org/), where I am the maintainer of [marK](https://invent.kde.org/education/mark), a dataset annotation tool.
